HOW TO USE METANOTES LIKE A PRO:

Here are some tips about how you can make MetaNotes.com yours.

1) Add pictures to your spaces fast!

If you see a picture on the Internet you like, why not include it in one of your spaces? You can easily make collections of images from all over the Web that you can share with anyone.

To place an image in a note, get its URL and just use this simple format: 

!URL!

To get an image's URL in Firefox, just right-click it to "Copy Image Location".

Then create a new note, and quickly type ! then [Ctrl-V] then ! then TAB. Don't enter any spaces in there! :)

2) Use bullet points to make lots of lists.

To make a bullet point list, use stars (aka asterisks - shift-8 on your keyboard): 

My Favorite Bands

* Stereolab
* The Wu-Tang Clan
* The Minutemen
* Unwound
* Black Sabbath

For sub-bullets, simply add more stars: 

To Do List

* Finish help docs
** Graphical
** Text
* Buy printer paper
** Get highlighters too

3) Make text in notes bigger than normal.

h1. This text will be Extra-Extra-Large.

h2. This text will be Extra-Large.

h3. This text will be Large.

This way you can add emphasis to a key phrase you want to call out on a space.

4) Bold and italicize your text.

Surround the text to bold with stars: 

Hey everyone!  I said, *HEY, EVERYONE!!!*

Use underscores to italicize bits of text: 

I am re-reading _Brave New World._

5) Link out to other Web pages.

The syntax is this: 

"Link Description":URL

For instance, 

"Buy Stickers":http://www.stickernation.com

6) Change the color of your notes !!!

Use the "contextual menu" that pops out of the icon on the bottom left of your screen and select the "color" menu item.

You'll need to understand HEX color code to change the color of a note. Some common colors are: 

Black = #000000
White = #FFFFFF
Yellow = #FFFF00
Red = #FF0000

6) Invite other users to collaborate.

Just send them the URL of the space! Once they get a free account, they can add notes to your spaces in real-time. That means you see their notes pop up and change while you watch. It's cool.

7) Use the TIME LOG !!!!

The Time Log is a great way to keep track of what you're going through. It's like a PRIVATE version of Twitter. Enter ideas or events in there as they occur. Then you'll get this cool history list which will help you remember just about everything that happens to you. I use the Time Log just about all the time. Just click the clock icon in the top right corner and log in with your MetaNotes password.

8) Use TWITTER !!!

Twitter is the CB radio of MetaNotes! Use Twitter to find your friends (near the bottom of the Twitter panel). Then answer the eternal question - "What are you doing right now?" All your Twitter followers will hear your broadcast.

To start an account, go to http://www.twitter.com.

9) Start TONS of spaces for ALL KINDS OF THINGS.

You can track just about any research topic in MetaNotes. We do all of our planning on MetaNotes and we believe it is why we're so effective as a collaborative team.

Remember, we're an open space: other people can surf into your pages, so expect collaboration!

10) Discover your friends' pages!!!

Get their username and point your browser to: http://www.metanotes.com/username

11) Bookmark your individual spaces!!!

You can create links in your Bookmarks Toolbar in your browser so that you can revisit certain spaces very fast while browsing elsewhere.

12) Use the GMAIL panel !!!

If you have a Gmail account, you can check your email while you work in MetaNotes. You can even copy-paste your emails into MetaNotes to keep a wall of important, timeless, or classic emails that you receive. It's a great way to remember them!
